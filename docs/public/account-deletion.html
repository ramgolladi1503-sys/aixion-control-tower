1. How to request deletion

You can request deletion of your Aixion Control Tower account in either of these ways.

  1. Inside the app: sign in, open Account, find Privacy and data controls, and tap Request account removal.
  2. Outside the app: email the privacy/support contact listed below with the subject Aixion account deletion request.

Important: Account deletion is not the same as logging out. Logging out only removes local access from your device. A deletion request starts account removal or anonymization processing.

2. What happens after an in-app request

When an authenticated in-app request is submitted, the backend currently performs these actions:

  • Records the account deletion request.
  • Revokes active sessions for the requesting user.
  • Disables account access.
  • Records an audit event named auth.account_deletion_requested.
  • Returns request status RECEIVED.

The app clears the local saved session after a successful request.

3. What data may be deleted or anonymized

Depending on the final operator retention policy and legal/security obligations, deletion or anonymization may cover:

  • Account profile data.
  • Active sessions.
  • Device tokens if notifications are enabled.
  • Invite records tied only to the account, where appropriate.
  • Personal identifiers in operational records where deletion or anonymization is possible.

4. What may be retained

Some records may be retained or anonymized rather than immediately deleted where needed for security, audit integrity, legal obligations, fraud/abuse prevention, backup recovery windows, or compliance.

Examples may include:

  • Audit logs required to prove approval decisions and controlled execution.
  • Security records related to access changes or session revocation.
  • Records required to investigate abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access.
  • Backups until the backup retention window expires.
